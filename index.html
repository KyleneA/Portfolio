Hello! Bonjour!

Welcome to my little corner of UX Design and Web Design

I'm Kylene Anne Aguila, a budding designer and UX Researcher from Montreal, Canada. I'm currently learning web design at Vanier College. I'm truly passionate about crafting designs that sincerely address real problems with a user-centric touch. My background in psychology and anthropology is my quiet strength, which gives me a unique approach to creating design solutions.

While I'm still finding my way in the vast realm of UX and Web Design, I am eager and genuinely excited to continue to learn and grow.

Join me on this journey of continuous improvement and creating designs that make a positive impact. I'm here to soak in every bit of knowledge and experience I possibly can.

About Me

Through my older sister, I developed a deep curiosity for the human experience. I pursued this curiosity and earned a First-Class Honors Degree in Psychology with Distinction and a minor in anthropology.

My position at the Scleroderma Patient-centered Intervention Network (SPIN) ignited my interest in product development and research application.

Thereafter, I pursued a master's degree under Dr. MacLeod and Dr. Rezzonico. I discovered a passion for impactful research. Diving into UX design through courses and the linGrow project in Dr. MacLeod's lab, I found the perfect fusion of research and design, sparking my unwavering commitment to a career in UX and design.

Acknowledging where my experience and learnings were lacking, I applied to and started the Web Design Specialist ACS program at Vanier College.
