About Me

After falling in love with psychology post-high school, I earned a First-Class Honors Degree in Psychology with Distinction and a minor in anthropology. A pivotal role at the Scleroderma Patient-centered Intervention Network (SPIN) ignited my interest in product development and research application. Pursuing a master's under Dr. MacLeod and Dr. Rezzonico, I discovered a passion for impactful research. Diving into UX design through courses and the linGrow project in Dr. MacLeod's lab, I found the perfect fusion of research and design, sparking my unwavering commitment to a career in UX.
